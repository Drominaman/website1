DeleteMe

DeleteMe is the original brand (in our opinion still the best on the market). It’s easy to set up, manage, and really works.

Good for

  • Simple onboarding
  • Taking you off the most dangerous data brokers

Pricing

DeleteMe starts at $8.71 /mo.

Available in the USA and Europe.

It automatically opts you out of dozens of people-search sites on a recurring basis and provides regular progress updates.

It blocks spam emails and features an easy, user friendly dashboard, with a free data checker scan and a family plan.

Trusted by investigators and legal professionals, DeleteMe is known for its simplicity and brand recognition. Though manual controls and refund guarantees are not offered they do help you opt out of any site they cannot automatically remove you from.

Incogni

Incogni is backed by Surfshark and praised on Reddit for its automated, large-scale opt-outs and clear reporting.

What makes Incogni a strong DeleteMe alternative?

  • Automated opt-outs across 150+ data brokers
  • Clean dashboard & progress tracking
  • Reputable backing by Surfshark

Pricing

Incogni starts at $8.29 /mo (billed annually).

Available in the USA, EU, UK, and Canada.

It automatically scans and removes listings from over 150 data brokers on a recurring basis, and even provides manual opt-out support where needed.

You’ll get clear progress updates, spam-email blocking, phishing detection, a free data-checker scan, a family-plan option, a money-back guarantee and 24/7 support—all wrapped in a clean, cloud-based dashboard (no mobile app).

With Trustpilot ratings north of 4.5, it’s a full-featured yet affordable choice for hands-off privacy maintenance.

Kanary

Kanary shines with deep broker coverage, high-accuracy detection, and both manual & automatic removal controls.

Highlights

  • Largest broker list & precise exposures
  • Manual removal controls
  • Free trial to test results

Pricing

Kanary starts at $14.99 /mo (20% off with code kzR9y7LX).

Available in the USA.

It offers deep broker coverage and precise exposure detection, with both automated recurring removals and manual opt-out controls.

Users receive clear progress updates, spam-email blocking and a family plan option.

Privacy-focused and highly configurable, Kanary is ideal if you value control over a steeper learning curve.

Optery

Optery offers a free initial scan and tiered plans that combine manual and automated removals.

  • Free scan included
  • Manual & automated options

Pricing

Optery starts at $3.99 /mo.

Available in the USA.

It includes a free initial scan and offers both automated recurring removals and manual opt-out capabilities.

Users receive progress updates, spam email blocking and a family plan option.

Super-low cost and flexible, Optery is an excellent choice for budget-conscious users seeking reliable privacy support.

Aura

Aura is a slick service that runs regularly removal reports for $12/year.

  • Wide range of features
  • Great UX

Pricing

Aura starts at $12 /mo (12% off site-wide sale).

Available in the USA.

It inspects people-search sites and provides regular status updates.

You also get spam-email blocking, phishing protection, a free data checker, a family plan option, a money-back guarantee and 24/7 support. All through Aura’s sleek interface.

As part of a broader security suite, Aura shines in UX and threat protection but does not offer automated recurring broker purges or manual opt-outs.

OneRep

OneRep is budget-friendly yet powerful, offering both manual-opt-out controls and automated recurring removals for DIY privacy maintenance.

  • Enables manual opt-out control
  • family options

Pricing

OneRep starts at $8.33 /mo.

Budget-friendly yet powerful, OneRep is a DIYer favorite—just be aware of its leadership’s apparent broker affiliations.

How These Services Work

Every data removal service follows a similar process:

  1. Scan known data brokers for your personal info.
  2. Generate opt out requests (automated or manual).
  3. Track each request until your profile is scrubbed or the record expires.
  4. Repeat scans periodically to catch any new re-listings.

What’s the best DeleteMe alternative for you?

Your ideal data-removal service depends on your priorities.

If you’re looking for the most hassle‑free, automated experience, Incogni is hard to beat. For budget‑conscious users who still want reliable opt‑outs, Optery offers low pricing and a free initial scan. If hands‑on control and manual removals matter most, consider OneRep. Fans of a well‑known brand and simple setup may prefer DeleteMe, while those who need added security features like phishing protection may lean toward Aura. Finally, if you need a balance of affordability, feature depth, and family‑plan options, Kanary makes an excellent middle ground.
